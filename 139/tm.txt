139. Subarray Sum Closest
中文
English
Given an integer array, find a subarray with sum closest to zero. Return the indexes of the first number and last number.

Example
Example1

Input: 
[-3,1,1,-3,5] 
Output: 
[0,2]
Explanation: [0,2], [1,3], [1,1], [2,2], [0,4]
Challenge
O(nlogn) time

92. Backpack
жпнд
English

Given n items with size Ai, an integer m denotes the size of a backpack. How full you can fill this backpack?
Example

Example 1:
	Input:  [3,4,8,5], backpack size=10
	Output:  9

Example 2:
	Input:  [2,3,5,7], backpack size=12
	Output:  12
	

Challenge

O(n x m) time and O(m) memory.
